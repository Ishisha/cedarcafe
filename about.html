So what exactly makes the Mediterranean diet so healthy? It all comes down to the fats! You might be surprised to learn that not all fats are created equally, and they're definitely not all bad. Making sure you're adding healthy fats to your diet, including olive oil, nuts and avocados, and staying away from unhealthy fats like butter and margarines are key when lowering your risk for heart disease and other cardiovascular issues. That's not to say we don't enjoy what we eat. It's all about moderation and making smart choices. The Mediterranean diet also includes whole grain breads, fruits, veggies, lean meats, and nuts and we season our food with natural herbs and spices instead dousing it with extra salt.
